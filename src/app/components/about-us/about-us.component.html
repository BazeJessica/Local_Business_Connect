Our Story

In 2020, we noticed a growing disconnect between local businesses and their communities. Small businesses struggled to reach customers online, while people found it increasingly difficult to discover and book local services they could trust.

We founded this platform with a simple belief: technology should bring communities together, not drive them apart. By creating an intuitive, reliable connection between businesses and customers, we're helping local economies thrive.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of businesses and hundreds of thousands of customers, facilitating millions of meaningful interactions that strengthen communities across the globe.
